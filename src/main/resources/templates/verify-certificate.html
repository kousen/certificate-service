Verify the Signature Cryptographically

You can also upload the PDF for a full cryptographic check of its digital signature: curl -F file=@certificate.pdf /api/certificates/verify reports whether the document is intact and was signed by this service. The signing certificate itself can be downloaded from /api/certificates/public-key and imported into your PDF reader's trusted identities.

About the Digital Signature

Each certificate is digitally signed with a 4096-bit RSA key using SHA-512 algorithm. While the certificate is self-signed (not issued by a central Certificate Authority), the signature mathematically guarantees that the PDF has not been altered since it was signed.
